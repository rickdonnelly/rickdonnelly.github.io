Amateur Radio

This has been a hobby for mine for several years. I am mostly into low power (<5 watts) digital communications on higher bands. My favorite targets for contacts are islands in the Indian Ocean. I keep my skills sharp by volunteering on a communications team with Southern Baptist Disaster Relief. Most of the musing I post here will be inspired by work in that realm.
